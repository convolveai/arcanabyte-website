Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our robotics consulting and deployment process

01

How long does it take to deploy a robot in our facility?

Our rapid deployment process typically takes 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to having a robot operational in your facility. This includes facility assessment, robot configuration, installation, and basic training. Pilot programs can be up and running even faster.

02

Do we need robotics expertise to work with your robots?

No robotics expertise required! Our robots are designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. We provide comprehensive training for your team, and our software solutions make robot management simple. Our 24/7 support team is always available to help with any questions.

03

What safety measures are in place for the robots?

Safety is our top priority. All our robots come with advanced collision detection, emergency stop systems, and comprehensive safety protocols. They're certified to meet industry safety standards and can operate safely alongside human workers. We conduct thorough safety assessments before deployment.

04

How much does it cost to get started with robotics?

We offer flexible pricing options starting with our pilot program, which requires minimal upfront investment. The pilot program lets you test robotics in your facility with no long-term commitment. Full deployment costs vary based on your specific needs and can be financed over time. Most clients see ROI within 6-12 months.

05

Can your robots integrate with our existing systems?

Yes! Our robots are designed for seamless integration with existing systems and workflows. We assess your current infrastructure during the consultation phase and customize the deployment to work with your existing equipment, software, and processes. No major infrastructure changes are typically required.
